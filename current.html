C4 Placement

For the 2022-2023 school year I am participating in York University's C4 placement program. C4 is the Cross-Campus Capstone Classroom, in which students work in multi-disciplinary teams to engage in project-based placement. In groups, students work closely with community partners on a project that creates change within their organization. For this C4 term, the group I am in will be partnering with Anne J MacArthur Public School to address the challenge question "How can Grade 6 students promote environmental education in their local community?"

I am hoping to gain project management experience during this group project and partnership.
